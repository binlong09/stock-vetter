## Reverse DCF — what is the current price assuming?

At a current price of $234.27 per share and trailing FCF of $139.5B, the reverse DCF asks: what 10-year FCF growth rate would justify this price under various assumptions?

The implied growth is below the historical pace — the market may be pricing in a deceleration or slowdown.
