## Reverse DCF — what is the current price assuming?

At a current price of $362.70 per share and trailing FCF of $1.6B, the reverse DCF asks: what 10-year FCF growth rate would justify this price under various assumptions?

Under the central assumptions (10% discount rate, 20× terminal P/FCF), the market is pricing in approximately 19.6% annual FCF growth for 10 years.
For comparison, actual FCF CAGR has been 30.9% over the past 5 years and 12.4% over the past 3 years.
The implied growth is below the historical pace — the market may be pricing in a deceleration or slowdown.
